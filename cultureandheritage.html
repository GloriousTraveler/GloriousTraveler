Cameron Highlands Culture & Heritage


The indigenous inhabitants of the Cameron Highlands, known as Orang Asli, are an essential component of the identity of the area. The Orang Asli, who are divided into several subgroups such as the Semai and Temiar, have unique languages, cultures, and customs that have been handed down through the centuries. They frequently reside in traditional communities and work in small-scale agriculture and handicrafts. The cultural and biological diversity of the Cameron Highlands is greatly enhanced by their enduring relationship with the land and their traditional environmental knowledge.
